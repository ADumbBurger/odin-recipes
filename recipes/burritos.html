Burritos

Description

These burritos I make are really good and I think they're healthy. It's made out of ingrediants from the store and I hear those are healthy so...

Ingrediants

  • Ground Beef (or Ground Lamb)
  • Garlic
  • Sweet Onion
  • Bell Peppers
  • Spinach
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Rice
  • Beef Broth

Directions

  1. Chop up onion, garlic, bell peppers, & spinach
  2. Melt butter in pan
  3. Add onion and garlic to pan
  4. Add meat to pan
  5. Add bell peppers
  6. Add rice and spinach
  7. Add beef broth
