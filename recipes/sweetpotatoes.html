Sweet Potatoes

Description

I eat these everyday, its like a natural relaxer. The only thing you need are ingrediants from the store!

Ingrediants

  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Chop up sweet potatoes and lay out on baking sheet
  3. Drizzle olive oil
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and cinnamon
  5. Season with more cinnamon
  6. Bake for 15 minutes then broil for 8 minutes
