Pancakes

Description

I dont really make pancakes the right way, but my way is healthier I think because I incorporate ingrediants from the store. I hear those are good for you.

Ingrediants

  • Pancake Mix
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Peanut Butter
  • Cinnamon

Directions

  1. Mix all ingrdiants besides butter in a bowl
  2. Melt butter in a pan
  3. Pour mixture into pan
  4. Flip
  5. Enjoy
